Nazi War Cabinet

Agenda : Planning the course of the War from the onset of Operation Barbarossa. (Freeze time: June 1941-1945)

Dear Delegates,

"It is not truth that matters, but victory."
Germany, June 1941, Operation Barbarossa, invasion of the Soviet Union.
150 divisions and three thousand tanks smashed across the frontier into Soviet territory. The decision to break the Treat of Non-Aggression was not a sudden one, it was deliberated and thus executed as an offensive, anti-communist attack. The largest invasion in the history of warfare.

The timeline for the Cabinet begins from the onset of Operation Barbarossa. The delegates, as ministers, deliberate and decide the course of action for the war.

As Hitler said, "If you want to shine like the sun, first you have to burn like it." So, go ahead, put yourself out there, plan, plot, organise the plan of action to be taken for the Cabinet, the glory of Nazi Germany, rests upon your shoulders.
Bis Bald.

All the best!
