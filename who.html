WHO

World Health Organisation

Agenda : Combatting Mental Disorders.

Dear Delegates,

As the Chairperson of World Health Organisation for Auxi MUN 2019, I feel immense pleasure in extending a warm welcome to you. Since 1948, the prestigious and active organisation of WHO has been working diligently to give effect to the international cause of promoting public health. It's success story covers the eradication of several deadly diseases on a global basis. This time the Auxi MUN has set "combatting mental health disorders" as the agenda to encourage awareness regarding the sensitivity of mental health as a condition of concern. Therefore, I look forward to voluntary participation, stimulating discussions and thought provoking debates.

All the best!
