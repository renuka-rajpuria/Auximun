UNSC

United Nations Security Council

Agenda : Dealing with the Sudan Crisis and its impact on peace security and development.

Dear Delegates,

The United Nations Security Council, since its very inception, is considered the highest shrine of international diplomacy. In AUXIMUN'19, the UNSC will be discussing the very pertinent Sudan Crisis, and its impact on peace security and development. Delegates will be dealing with real time crises as diplomats. The work of diplomats continue even when soldiers lay down their rifles and make their way back through war-torn lands.

All the best!
