About Us

Auxilium Convent School,Dum Dum.

Through its continued dedication to pioneering education and maintaining school traditions, Auxilium Convent School,Dum Dum has earned its reputation as one of the best girls school in Kolkata. The school is run by Daughters of Mary Help of Christians and is a reputed part of the Schools of Salesian Sisters of Don Bosco. It is a school offering educational programmmes through three faculties:- Humanities, Commerce and Science. It is affiliated to the Indian School Certificate Education Board (ICSE and ISC) based in New Delhi.



About AUXIMUN

We believe that Model United Nations enables young minds to form a perspective on global problems and understand them from different point of views. It is the perfect platform for students to address matters of vitality and express their opinions about global activities of concern, be it the various armed conflicts around the world, issues of human rights, health and medical issues or issues relating to economic relations. At AUXIMUN 2019 we will simulate FOUR committees to enable students to discuss issues of different concerns affecting different regions of the world.
